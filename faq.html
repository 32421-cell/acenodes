Frequently Asked Questions

What Minecraft versions do you support?

We support all official Minecraft versions including Vanilla, Spigot, Bukkit, PaperMC, Forge, and Fabric.

Can I install custom mods and plugins?

Yes! Our control panel allows you to upload and manage mods/plugins easily with full FTP access.

Do you offer DDoS protection?

All plans come with enterprise-grade DDoS protection at no extra cost.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, upgrading your plan is easy and your data will be migrated seamlessly.

Do you have a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new customers.

How do I contact support?

We offer 24/7 live chat and ticket support accessible from your control panel or our contact page.
